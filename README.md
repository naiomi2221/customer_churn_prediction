# customer_churn_prediction
Logistic Regression Model

Installed the necessary libraries to test and train the data.
This was designed to predict the probability that a customer 
will churn or leave the company and what factors play a part
in that decision.

Findings: Customers who were at high risk for churn were 
with the company for a short period of time. They were also 
at higher risk while using Fiber-Optics and/or paperless billing.

dependencies are:
Pandas 
Numpy
Matplotlib
Seaborn
Scikit-learn
